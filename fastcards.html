Flashcards are great. Creating flashcards isn't.

Spaced repetition flashcards are the gold standard for vocab acquisiton - but building out new decks is tedious. It's become a mental hurdle that's slowing down my progress.

I'm building a tool to speed up flashcard creation. Start by pasting Spanish text on the page. Hover over any word to see a translation instantly. Click on the word to see all of its translations. Double click to instantly produce a flashcard. Export cards to any common flashcard app - Anki, Mochi, etc.
